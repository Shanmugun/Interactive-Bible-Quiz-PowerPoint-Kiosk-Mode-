# 🎯 Interactive Bible Quiz - PowerPoint (Kiosk Mode)

An interactive **Bible Quiz PowerPoint presentation** designed for youth meetings, church gatherings, and fellowship events. This presentation runs fully in **Kiosk Mode**, allowing users to navigate using clickable buttons and interactive elements.

📅 **Event:** YAF - March 1st, 2026  
📽️ Run in: **Slide Show → Set Up Slide Show → Browsed at a kiosk (Full Screen)**

---

## 🚀 How It Works

### 1️⃣ Start the Quiz
- On the first slide, click **"START THE QUIZ"**.
- This navigates to the **"Pick a Number"** slide.

---

### 2️⃣ Pick a Number
- Click any available number to open the corresponding question.
- Once a number is selected:
  - The question opens.
  - The selected number disappears from the "Pick a Number" slide.
  - That question cannot be selected again.

---

### 3️⃣ Question Slide Features

Each question slide contains:

#### ⏱ 15-Second Timer
- Timer is displayed on the **top left corner**.
- To start the timer, click the **logo on the top right corner**.
- Timer functionality is available on:
  - Question-only slide
  - Question-with-options slide

---

### 4️⃣ Reveal Options
- Click on the **question text** to reveal the answer options.

---

### 5️⃣ Select an Answer
- Click on any option to check your answer:
  - ✅ Correct answer turns **Green**
  - ❌ Wrong answer turns **Red**

---

### 🏠 Navigation After Correct Answer
- Once the correct option is selected:
  - A **Home icon** appears at the bottom middle.
  - Click it to return to:
    - "Pick a Number" slide
    - Or Home slide

You can continue selecting remaining numbers until all questions are completed.

---

### ❌ End the Quiz
- After completing all questions:
  - Click the **“X” (close icon)** next to the **"Pick a Number"** title.
  - This navigates to the final slide.

---

### 🎉 Final Slide
The quiz ends with a celebratory closing slide:

> **PRAISE THE LORD**

---

## ⚙️ Important Setup Instructions

Before running the quiz:

1. Go to **Slide Show**
2. Click **Set Up Slide Show**
3. Select:
   - ✅ *Browsed at a kiosk (Full screen)*

⚠️ This ensures:
- Slides cannot be advanced manually
- Navigation works only via clickable buttons
- Full interactive experience
